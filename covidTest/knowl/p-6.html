All our previous explorations of the logistic model relied on graphs that had \(P\) on the horizontal axis. However, in practice the values for \(N\) are reported daily or weekly so it is much more natural to consider the graph of \(N\) plotted against time. In fact almost all the graphs we see in the media or data sites are of \(P\) or \(N\) plotted against time. Figure \ref{f:Nvtexample} is one such example.
