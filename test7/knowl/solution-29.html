Solution 7.2.1.

Using the log rules,

\begin{equation*} \newcommand{\amp}{\amp }\begin{aligned} \displaystyle {1.04}^t \amp= {5}\\ \log{({1.04})^t} \amp= \log {5}\\ t\log{1.04} \amp= \log {5}\\ t \amp= \frac{\log{5}}{\log{1.04}}. \end{aligned} \end{equation*}

Using your calculator, you will find that

\(\frac{\log{5}}{\log{1.04}}\approx {41}\text{.}\) You can check your answer: \({1.04}^{{41}}\approx{5}\text{.}\)
