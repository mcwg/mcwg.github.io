It is important to always keep in mind that some systems use the notation \(\log =\ln\text{.}\) A quick way to test if this is the case is to compute \(\log 10\text{.}\) If you get \(1\text{,}\) then \(\log\) is the base-ten log as defined above. If you get \(2.30255...\) then \(\log\) is the natural log in your system!
