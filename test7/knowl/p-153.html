One just needs to use the relations that come from the fact that logs and exponentials are inverses:

\begin{equation*} \log 10^x=x, \qquad \text{and}\qquad \ln e^x=x, \end{equation*}

which allow you to bring the variable in an equation “down from an exponent”. You just need to apply logs to both sides of the equation (if two things are equal, then their logs are equal too!).
