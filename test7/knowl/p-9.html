It usually is a mistake to try to memorize the definition of logs without any understanding of what is going on. The symbols “jump around” too much from one equation to the other in

\begin{equation*} y=\log x \quad\text{ means }\quad 10^y=x \end{equation*}

for pure memory to not fail you in the future (specially since \(10^y=x\) can equally well be written in the form \(x=10^y\)).
