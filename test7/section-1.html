Section 1 Goals

After completing this worksheet, you should be able to:

  • Define the common and natural logarithms as exponents and use this definition to find exact values of these functions.
  • Interpret a logarithm as an inverse function of an exponential function.
  • Use the log properties to simplify expressions.
  • Use logs and their properties to solve exponential equations.
