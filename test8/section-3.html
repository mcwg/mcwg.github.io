Section 3 First set: Algebraic structure - converting between the forms

Each of the forms \(Q=ab^t\) and \(Q=ae^{kt}\) is useful in specific contexts, so make sure you can convert between the two forms using algebra.

It is not a good idea to memorize the way to convert between the two forms. Whatever is memorized will most likely be soon forgotten.

Just set \(ae^{kt}=ab^t\) and solve for whatever you need!
