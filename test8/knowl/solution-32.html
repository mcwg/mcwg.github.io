Solution 7.5.1.

Using the log rules,

\begin{equation*} \newcommand{\amp}{\amp }\begin{aligned} \displaystyle {1.08}^t \amp= {4}\\ \log{({1.08})^t} \amp= \log {4}\\ t\log{1.08} \amp= \log {4}\\ t \amp= \frac{\log{4}}{\log{1.08}}. \end{aligned} \end{equation*}

Using your calculator, you will find that

\(\frac{\log{4}}{\log{1.08}}\approx {18}\text{.}\) You can check your answer: \({1.08}^{{18}}\approx{4}\text{.}\)
