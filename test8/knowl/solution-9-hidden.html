Let \(t\) be the doubling time. Then the population is \(2P_0\) at time \(t \text{,}\) so

\begin{equation*} \newcommand{\amp}{\amp }\begin{aligned} 2P_0 \amp = P_0 e^{{{0.3} }t} \\ 2 \amp = e^{{{0.3} }t} \\ {{0.3} } t \amp = \ln 2 \\ t \amp = \frac{\ln 2}{{{0.3} }}\approx 2.310.\ \end{aligned} \end{equation*}

So, the doubling time is about \(2.3\) years.
