Solution 6.2.1.

We have \(P=ab^t\) where \(a={{7.4} }\) and \(b={{1.019}}\text{.}\) We want to find \(k\) such that

\begin{equation*} P={{7.4}} e^{kt}={{7.4}}({{1.019}})^t, \end{equation*}

so

\begin{equation*} e^k = {{1.019}}. \end{equation*}

Thus, the continuous growth rate is \(k=\ln{{1.019}}\approx {0.01882}\text{,}\) or \({{1.882}}\%\) per year.
