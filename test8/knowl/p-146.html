Let \(Q(t)\) be the mass of the substance at time \(t \text{,}\) and \(Q_0\) be the initial mass of the substance. Since the substance is decaying at a continuous rate, we know that \(Q(t)=Q_0e^{kt}\) where \(k=-{0.1}\) (The decay is \({10} \%\)). So \(Q(t)=Q_0e^{-{0.1} t} \text{.}\) We want to know when \(Q(t)=\frac{1}{2}Q_0 \text{.}\)
