Solution 5.6.1.

  1. We have \({\rm curl} (\vec{F})(x,y,z)= (0 - 0)\vec{i} - (0 - 0)\vec{j} + (0 - 0)\vec{k} = \vec{0}\text{,}\) and thus \({\rm curl} (\vec{F})(0,0,0)=\vec{0}\text{.}\)

  2. We have \({\rm div} (\vec{F})(x,y,z)= 1 + 0 + 0 = 1\text{,}\) and thus \({\rm div} (\vec{F})(0,0,0)=1\text{.}\)
