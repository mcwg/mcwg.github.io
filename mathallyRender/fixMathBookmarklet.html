Fix-Math Bookmarklet

Drag the link below to your bookmarks bar (enable it in the "view" menu if not visible) to save the Fix-Math bookmarklet:

Fix-Math!

Once saved, you can use this bookmarklet on any website to render LaTeX math enclosed in \(...\) or \[...\].

How to Use

  1. Save the bookmarklet as described above.
  2. Visit a website with LaTeX math (e.g., \(E=mc^2\) or \[\int_0^\infty e^{-x^2} dx\]).
  3. Click the Fix-Math! bookmarklet in your bookmarks bar to render the math.
