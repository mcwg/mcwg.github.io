False.

The formula for integration by parts does not help us find \(\displaystyle\int uv\,dx\text{.}\) However, it does help us find \(\displaystyle \int uv'\,dx.\) Although this is the integral of the product of two functions, the functions are \(u\) and \(v'\text{,}\) not \(u\) and \(v\text{.}\)
