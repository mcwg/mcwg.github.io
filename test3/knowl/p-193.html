This lets us write a formula for the less familiar integral:

\begin{align*} \underbrace{\int e^x\,dx}_{\text{more familiar}}+\underbrace{\int xe^x\,dx}_{\text{less familiar}}\amp =xe^x+C\\ \underbrace{\int xe^x\,dx}_{\text{less familiar}}\amp =xe^x-\underbrace{\int e^x\,dx}_{\text{more familiar}} \\ \amp =xe^x-e^x+C. \end{align*}
