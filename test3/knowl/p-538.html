In integration by parts, once we chose \(u\) and \(v'\text{,}\) we need to find \(u'\) and \(v\text{.}\) It is usually possible to find \(u'\) from \(u\text{,}\) but it can be hard to find \(v\) from \(v'\text{.}\) However, once we are able to find \(v\text{,}\) we are done: Integration by parts will always let us find an answer, provided we can find a formula for \(v\text{.}\)
