Section 5 More abstract implications

Subsection 5.1 (**) PENDING

Formulas about integrals --- relating some integrals to others. For example:

\begin{equation*} \int_a^b e^{kt}\, dt = \frac{1}{k}\int_{ka}^{kb} e^w \, dw \end{equation*}

Point is to show that this has an importance even uf something like wolfram alpha exists.
