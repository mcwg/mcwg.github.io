Since \(x=u\) and \(y=v\) in the parameterization for \(S\text{,}\) this is the graph of a function. Substituting \(u=x\) and \(v=y\) in the equation for \(z=\sqrt{u^2+v^2}\text{,}\) we get \(z=\sqrt{x^2+y^2}\text{,}\) so \(S\) must be the graph of the upward-facing cone \(z=\sqrt{x^2+y^2}\text{.}\)
