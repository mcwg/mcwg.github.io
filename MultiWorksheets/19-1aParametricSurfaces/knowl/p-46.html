Since \(x=u\) and \(y=v\) in the parameterization for \(S\text{,}\) this is the graph of a function. Substituting \(u=x\) and \(v=y\) in the equation for \(z=\sqrt{1-u^2-v^2}\text{,}\) we get \(z=\sqrt{1-x^2-y^2}\text{,}\) so \(S\) is the graph of \(z=\sqrt{1-x^2-y^2}\text{.}\) Squaring both sides and moving all variables to the left-hand side, we get \(x^2+y^2+z^2=1\text{,}\) so \(S\) is the top half of the sphere \(x^2+y^2+z^2=1\) with \(z\geq 0\text{.}\)
