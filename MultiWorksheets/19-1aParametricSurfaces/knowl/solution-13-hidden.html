The cylinder \({25}=x^2+y^2\) has cross sections which consist of circles centered on the \(z\)-axis whose radii are all equal to \({5}\text{.}\) Therefore, it can be parameterized by letting \(z=v\text{,}\) \(x={5}\cos{(u)}\) and \(y={5}\sin{(u)}\text{,}\) or \(\vec{r}(u,v) ={5}\cos{(u)} \vec{i} +{5}\sin{(u)} \vec{j} +v\vec{k}\text{.}\)
