Item 6.10.b.

We have

\begin{equation*} \newcommand{\amp}{\amp }\begin{aligned} \vec{N} \amp = \vec{r}_{u} \times \vec{r}_{v} \\ \amp = \left( \vec{i} +\frac{u}{\sqrt{u^2+v^2}} \vec{k} \right) \times \left( \vec{j} +\frac{v}{\sqrt{u^2+v^2}}\vec{k} \right) \\ \amp = -\frac{u}{\sqrt{u^2+v^2}} \vec{i} -\frac{v}{\sqrt{u^2+v^2}}\vec{j} +\vec{k}. \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
