Item 7.4.a.

In a parameterizations \(\vec{r}(u,v) =f(u,v)\vec{i} +g(u,v)\vec{j} +h(u,v)\vec{k}\text{,}\) we have \(x=f(u,v)\text{,}\) \(y=g(u,v)\) and \(z=h(u,v)\text{.}\) Use the given restrictions on \(x\text{,}\) \(y\) and \(z\) and these equations to find the corresponding restrictions on \(u\) and \(v\text{.}\)
