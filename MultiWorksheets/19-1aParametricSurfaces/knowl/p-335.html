Since \(x=u\text{,}\) \(y=v\) and \(z=\sqrt{u^2+v^2}\text{,}\) in order to have \(z\leq {3}\text{,}\) we must have \(\sqrt{u^2+v^2}\leq {3}\) or equivalently \(u^2+v^2\leq {9}\text{.}\) This is all points within the circle \(u^2+v^2\leq {9}\text{,}\) and thus can be bounded by either \(-{3}\leq u\leq {3}\) and \(-\sqrt{{9}-u^2} \leq v\leq \sqrt{{9}-u^2}\text{,}\) or \(-{3}\leq v\leq {3}\) and \(-\sqrt{{9}-v^2} \leq u\leq \sqrt{{9}-v^2}\text{.}\)
