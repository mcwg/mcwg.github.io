Solution 6.7.1.

Since this surface is the graph of \(y={\sqrt{4x^{2}+3z^{2}}}\text{,}\) we can choose \(x=u\text{,}\) \(z=v\) and we have \(y={\sqrt{4u^{2}+3v^{2}}}\text{.}\) Thus \(\vec{r}(u,v) =u\vec{i} +{\sqrt{4u^{2}+3v^{2}}}\,\vec{j} +v\vec{k}\) is a parameterization for this surface.
