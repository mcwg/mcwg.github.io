Item 7.4.a.
Since \(x=u\text{,}\) \(y=v\) and \(z=\sqrt{u^2+v^2}\text{,}\) in order to have \(z\leq \text{,}\) we must have \(\sqrt{u^2+v^2}\leq \) or equivalently \(u^2+v^2\leq \text{.}\) This is all points within the circle \(u^2+v^2\leq \text{,}\) and thus can be bounded by either \(-\leq u\leq \) and \(-\sqrt{-u^2} \leq v\leq \sqrt{-u^2}\text{,}\) or \(-\leq v\leq \) and \(-\sqrt{-v^2} \leq u\leq \sqrt{-v^2}\text{.}\)
