Since this surface is the graph of \(y={\sqrt{4x^{2}+5z^{2}}}\text{,}\) we can choose \(x=u\text{,}\) \(z=v\text{,}\) and we have \(y={\sqrt{4u^{2}+5v^{2}}}\text{.}\) Thus \(\vec{r}(u,v) =u\vec{i} +{\sqrt{4u^{2}+5v^{2}}}\,\vec{j} +v\vec{k}\) is a parameterization for this surface.
