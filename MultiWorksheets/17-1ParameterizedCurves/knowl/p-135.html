A circle with radius \(R\) centered at the point \((a,b)\) and oriented counter-clockwise is parameterized by \(\vec{r}(t) =(R\cos (t) +a)\vec{i} +(R\sin (t) +b)\vec{j}\text{.}\) To restrict it to the half-circle, find the values of the endpoints of the curve and the values of \(t\) that give those points.
