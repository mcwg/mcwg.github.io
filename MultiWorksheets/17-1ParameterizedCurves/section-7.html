Section 7 Wrap Up Questions

  1. Decide whether each of the following statements is true or false. Give a reason for your answer.

    1. Any parameterization of a circle has to include trigonometric functions.
    2. The vector equations \(\vec{r} (t)=t\vec{i}+t^2\vec{j}\) and \(\vec{r}(t) =\ln{(t)} \vec{i} +(\ln{(t)})^2 \vec{j}\) parameterize the same curve.
    3. To switch the orientation of a parameterization, we can replace \(t\) by \(-t\text{.}\)
  2.
