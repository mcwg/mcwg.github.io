First, \(S\) can be parameterized by \(\vec{r}(u,v)=u\vec{i} +v\vec{j} +(6-u-v)\vec{k}\text{.}\) Since we want to restrict to the first octant, we need \(x=u\geq 0\text{,}\) \(y=v\geq 0\text{,}\) \(z=6-u-v\geq 0\text{,}\) which gives the bounds \(0\leq u\leq 6\) and \(0\leq v\leq 6-u\text{.}\) The normal vector is \(\vec{N} =\vec{i} +\vec{j} +\vec{k}\text{,}\) so \(||N||=\sqrt{3}\text{.}\) Thus, we have

\begin{equation*} \newcommand{\amp}{\amp }\begin{aligned} \int \int_{S} xz \, dS \amp =\int_{0}^{6} \int_{0}^{6-u} u(6-u-v) \sqrt{3} \,dv\,du\\ \amp =\sqrt{3} \int_{0}^{6} \int_{0}^{6-u} (6u-u^2-uv) \,dv\,du \\ \amp =\sqrt{3} \int_{0}^{6} \left( 6uv-u^2v-u\frac{v^2}{2} \right)\bigg|_{0}^{6-u} \,du \\ \amp =\sqrt{3} \int_{0}^{6} \left( \frac{u^3}{2} -6u^2+{18}u \right) \,du \\ \amp =\sqrt{3} \left( \frac{u^4}{8} -{2u^{3}} +{9}u^2 \right)\bigg|_{0}^{6} \\ \amp =\sqrt{3} \left( \frac{6^4}{8} -2 (6)^3 +9\cdot 6^2 \right) \\ \amp = 54 \sqrt{3}. \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
