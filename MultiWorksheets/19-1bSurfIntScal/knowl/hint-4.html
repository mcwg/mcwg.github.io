Hint 5.2.1.

Note that by definition

\begin{equation*} \int \int_{S} f(x,y,z)\,dS =\int \int_{D} f(\vec{r}(u,v)) \,||\vec{N}|| \,du\,dv, \end{equation*}

where \(\vec{r}(u,v)\) is a parameterization of \(S\) and \(D\) is the corresponding region in the \(uv\)-plane. Can you figure out the sign of this last integral using only what you know about \(f\) without finding \(\vec{r}(u,v)\) and \(D\) explicitly?
