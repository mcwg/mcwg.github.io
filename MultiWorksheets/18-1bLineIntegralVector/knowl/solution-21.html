Solution 4.4.1.

A parametric curve trace out a unit circle if it satisfies the equation \(x^2 +y^2=1\text{.}\) All options, except 2, satisfy the equation.

The curves 1, 3, and 4 in the first part trace the circle in different speeds, which let them go through different portions of the circle more than once. Also, the curve number 3 has an increasing speed, since the \(t\) is squared.
