Item 4.3.a.

Since \(f_x={3e^{x}\cos\!\left(y\right)}\text{,}\) we have \(f_{xx}={3e^{x}\cos\!\left(y\right)}\) and \(f_{xy}={-3e^{x}\sin\!\left(y\right)}\text{.}\) Next, since \(f_y={-3e^{x}\sin\!\left(y\right)}\text{,}\) we have \(f_{yy}={-3e^{x}\cos\!\left(y\right)}\) and \(f_{yx}={-3e^{x}\sin\!\left(y\right)}\text{.}\) Observe that \(f_{xy}={-3e^{x}\sin\!\left(y\right)}=f_{yx}\text{.}\)
