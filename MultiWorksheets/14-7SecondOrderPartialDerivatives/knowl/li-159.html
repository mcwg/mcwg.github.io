Item 5.1.b.

If the point \((a,b)\) is a minimum of \(f(x,y)\text{,}\) then it is a minimum in the \(x\) and \(y\)-directions. This means \(f_{x}(a,b)=f_{y}(a,b)=0\text{,}\) and \(f_{xx}(a,b)\) and \(f_{yy}(a,b)\) are both positive (since the graph has to be concave up in both the \(x\) and \(y\)-directions).
