1. We have

    \begin{equation*} \text{curl}\,\vec{F}(x,y,z)= (0 - 0)\vec{i} - (0 - 0)\vec{j} + (-1 - 1)\vec{k} = -2\vec{k}\text{,} \end{equation*}

    and thus

    \begin{equation*} \text{curl}\,\vec{F}(0,0,0)=-2\vec{k}\text{.} \end{equation*}

  2. We have

    \begin{equation*} {\rm div} (\vec{F})(x,y,z)= 0 + 0 + 0 = 0\text{,} \end{equation*}

    and thus

    \begin{equation*} {\rm div} (\vec{F})(0,0,0)=0\text{.} \end{equation*}
