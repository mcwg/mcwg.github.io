Cartesian Coordinate Definition of the Divergence. For \(\vec{F} =F_1\vec{i} +F_2\vec{j} +F_3\vec{k}\) we define

\begin{equation*} {\rm div} \vec{F}=\frac{\partial F_1}{\partial x} +\frac{\partial F_2}{\partial y} +\frac{\partial F_3}{\partial z}. \end{equation*}

More generally, for a vector field \(\vec{F}\) in any dimension, we define \({\rm div} \vec{F} =\nabla \cdot \vec{F}\text{.}\)
