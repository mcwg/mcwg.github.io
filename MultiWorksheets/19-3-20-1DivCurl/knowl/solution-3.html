Solution 4.2.1.

  1. The vectors in \(\vec{F}\) rotate counter clockwise around the origin, so we expect the scalar curl of \(\vec{F}\) at \((0,0)\) to be greater than \(0\text{.}\)

  2. Since none of the vectors in \(\vec{F}\) point into or out of the origin, we expect \({\rm div} (\vec{F})(0,0)=0\text{.}\)
