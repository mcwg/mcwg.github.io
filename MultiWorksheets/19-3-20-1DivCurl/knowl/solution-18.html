Solution 5.6.1.

  1. We have

    \begin{equation*} \text{curl}\,\vec{F}(x,y,z)= (0 - 0)\vec{i} - (0 - 0)\vec{j} + (0 - 0)\vec{k} = \vec{0}\text{,} \end{equation*}

    and thus

    \begin{equation*} \text{curl}\,\vec{F}(0,0,0)=\vec{0}\text{.} \end{equation*}

  2. We have

    \begin{equation*} {\rm div} (\vec{F})(x,y,z)= 1 + 0 + 0 = 1\text{,} \end{equation*}

    and thus

    \begin{equation*} {\rm div} (\vec{F})(0,0,0)=1\text{.} \end{equation*}
