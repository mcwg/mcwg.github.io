Item 1.

Are the following statements true or false? Give reasons for your answers.

  1. A simple-closed curve is both simple and closed.

  2. It is possible to find two different parameterizations for a curve \(C\text{,}\) one of which is differentiable and one which is not differentiable.

  3. If a curve \(C\) parameterized by \(\vec{r}(t)\) is differentiable, then it is continuous.
