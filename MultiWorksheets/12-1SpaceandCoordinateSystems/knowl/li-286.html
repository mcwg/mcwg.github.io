Item 5.5.b.

For a point \(P\) to have a coordinate \(\theta =\pi /2\text{,}\) the projection of that point into the \(xy\)-plane has to lie on the \(y\)-axis with \(x\geq 0\text{.}\) Thus the surface \(\theta =\pi /2\) is the set of all points which share this property, which is geometrically a half-plane.
