Since the only restriction given is \(\rho ={1}\text{,}\) this is the set of all points a distance of \({1}\) from the origin. Geometrically, this is a sphere of radius \({1}\) centered at the origin. Converting to Cartesian coordinates, this equation becomes \(x^2+y^2+z^2={1}\text{,}\) which, as already noted, is a sphere of radius \({1}\) centered at the origin. This matches Figure A.
