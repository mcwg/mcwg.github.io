For \(x=0\) and \(x={3}\text{,}\) we get two vertical planes parallel to the \(yz\)-plane, for \(y=-{2}\) and \(y={2}\text{,}\) we get two vertical planes parallel to the \(xz\)-plane, and for \(z={3}\) and \(z={4}\text{,}\) we get two horizontal planes. It follows that the solid region \(0\leq x\leq {3}\text{,}\) \(-{2}\leq y\leq {2}\text{,}\) \({3}\leq z\leq {4}\) is in the shape of a box with sides parallel to the three coordinate planes.
