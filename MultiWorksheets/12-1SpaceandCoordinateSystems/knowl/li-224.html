Item 5.3.a.

Since the variable \(r\) measures the distance of a point from the \(z\)-axis, any point that satisfies the equation \(r={3}\) lies a distance \({3}\) from the \(z\)-axis. Thus the graph of the equation \(r={3}\) is a cylinder of radius \({3}\) centered on the \(z\)-axis. This matches Figure C.
