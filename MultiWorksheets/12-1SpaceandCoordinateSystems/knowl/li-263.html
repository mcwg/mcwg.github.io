Item 5.4.d.

The bounds \(0\leq r\leq {3}\) in the \(xy\)-plane are all points in the circle of radius \({3}\) centered at the origin. Next, since \(0\leq \theta \leq \pi /2 \text{,}\) this region must occur in the first \(xy\)-quadrant. Finally, since \(0 \leq z\leq {2} \text{,}\) the height of the region is bounded below by the plane \(z=0\) and above by the plane \(z={2} \text{.}\) In particular, this is a quarter of a cylinder of radius \({3}\) and height \({2}\) centered on the \(z\)-axis.
