Item 5.6.d.

First, since \(0\leq \rho\leq {1}\text{,}\) this region occurs inside the sphere of radius \({1}\) centered at the origin. Next, since \(0\leq \theta \leq \pi\text{,}\) this region must occur when \(y\geq 0\text{.}\) Finally, since \(\pi /2 \leq \phi \leq \pi\text{,}\) the region must have \(z\leq 0\text{.}\) In particular, this is the part of the solid sphere of radius \({1}\) centered at the origin that occurs where \(y\geq 0\) and \(z\leq 0\text{.}\) This matches Figure C.
