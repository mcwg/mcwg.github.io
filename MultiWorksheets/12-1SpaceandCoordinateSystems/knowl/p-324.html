For a point \(P\) to have a coordinate \(\phi =\pi /4\text{,}\) the line connecting the point to the origin has to make an angle of \(\pi /4\) with the positive \(z\)-axis. Thus the surface \(\phi =\pi /4\) is the set of all points which share this property, which is geometrically an upward-facing cone with vertex at the origin.
