Item 5.3.3.
If we fix the angle \(\theta=\pi /2\) in the \(xy\)-plane and allow \(r\) to vary, we obtain the \(y\)-axis with \(x\geq 0\text{.}\) Since \(z\) is also allowed to vary, this equation gives a vertical half-plane whose intersection with the \(xy\)-plane is the \(y\)-axis with \(x\geq 0\text{.}\) This matches Figure D.
