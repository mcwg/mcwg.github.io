Solution 4.4.1.

First, the magnitude of \(\vec{v} =\langle{-1},{2},{-1}\rangle\) is \(||\vec{v}|| =\sqrt{({-1})^2+({2})^2+({-1})^2} =\sqrt{{6}}\text{.}\) Then a unit vector pointing in the same direction as \(\vec{v}\) is

\begin{equation*} \frac{\vec{v}}{||\vec{v}||} =\frac{1}{\sqrt{{6}}} \langle{-1},{2},{-1}\rangle = \left\langle \frac{{-1}}{\sqrt{{6}}} , \frac{{2}}{\sqrt{{6}}} , \frac{{-1}}{\sqrt{{6}}} \right\rangle. \end{equation*}
