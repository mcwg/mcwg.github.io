The standard basis vectors for \({\mathbb R}^{3}\) are \(\vec{i}\text{,}\) \(\vec{j}\) and \(\vec{k}\text{,}\) the unit vectors pointing in the direction of the positive \(x\text{,}\) \(y\) and \(z\)-axis respectively. If \(\vec{v} =( a,b,c)\text{,}\) then it can also be written as \(a\vec{i} +b\vec{j} +c\vec{k}\)
