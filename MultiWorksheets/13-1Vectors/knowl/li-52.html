Item 4.2.a.

The vector \(\vec{PQ}\) has its tail at \(P\) and head to \(Q\text{.}\) The components are the difference in coordinates of the points \(P\) and \(Q\text{,}\) so \(\vec{PQ}=\langle -1-(-{2}),{1}-1,{4}-{3}\rangle=\langle{1},{0},{1}\rangle \text{.}\) Thus

\begin{equation*} ||\vec{PQ}||=\sqrt{({1})^2+({0})^2+({1})^2} =\sqrt{{2}}. \end{equation*}
