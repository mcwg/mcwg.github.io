Hint 4.1.1.

  1. The vector with tail at \(P=(a,b,c)\) and head at \(Q=(d,e,f)\) is \(\vec{PQ}=\langle d-a,e-b,f-c\rangle\text{.}\)

  2. The vector with tail at \(Q=(a,b,c)\) and head at \(P=(d,e,f)\) is \(\vec{QP}=\langle d-a,e-b,f-c \rangle \text{.}\)

  3. If \(\vec{v} =\langle v_{1},v_2,v_3\rangle\) has tail at point \(P=(a_1,a_2,a_3)\text{,}\) then its head is at the point \((a_1+v_1,a_2+v_2,a_3+v_3)\)
