Item 4.2.b.

If \(\vec{v}+\vec{w}\) has its tail placed at \(P\text{,}\) the coordinates of the point at its head will be the sum of the coordinates of \(Q\) with the components of \(\vec{v}+\vec{w}\text{.}\) We have \(\vec{v}+\vec{w}=\langle {-5},{8},{5}\rangle\) and so the point is \((-{2}+({-5}),1+{8},{3}+{5})=({-7},{9},{8})\text{.}\)
