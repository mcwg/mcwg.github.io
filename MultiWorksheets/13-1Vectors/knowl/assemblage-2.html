We say that two vectors \(\vec{v}\) and \(\vec{u}\) are parallel if one is a non-zero scalar multiple of the other i.e. there exists a scalar \(C\neq 0\) such that \(C\vec{u} =\vec{v}\text{.}\) Two parallel vectors are pointing in the same direction if \(C\) is positive and opposite directions if \(C\) is negative.
