Unless you are trying to keep something within your set of dollar signs out of displaystyle (a very rare occurrence), you should always, when there is a need for displaystyle, put the \dsty immediately after the first dollar sign. \dsty continues to remain active until it is delimited, usually by the next dollar sign. Although you can use {\dsty blah} within a set of dollar signs to limit its effect, this is very rarely useful and mostly makes an annoyance later.
