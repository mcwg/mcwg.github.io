Another thing you will need to adjust is the row \begin{calctabular}{c|c|c|c|c}. The first c is for the column containing the names of the variables or the function, and the remaining ones are for the entries in the table, so there should be as many of them as columns in the table (five in this case). Also, the c (center the column) could be replaced by l (left justify the column), or r (right justify the column).
